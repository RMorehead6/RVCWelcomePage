Welcome to

Welcome to Rock Valley College! We're excited that you have decided to pursue your educational and career goals with us and we look forward to having you on campus! Our friendly staff will help you be successful in your college career.

As a new student, the transition to college can be overwhelming. College is very different to high school, and it is critical to build a foundation for success during your first year of college. Typically, this means students switch from studying mainly in class to studying both in class and outside of class equally. Homework becomes less important, and tests become a large part of your final grade. Students also must get used to doing online homework through Canvas, an online learning management tool. A disasterous first year can turn a student off of college all together. Let us help you with the process as you begin your journey here at Rock Valley College.

Our Mission: Rock Valley College empowers the community to grow as a society of learners through well-designed educational pathways, leading to further education, rewarding careers, cultural enrichment, and economic-technological development.

What Do Current and Past Students Think About Rock Valley College?

Here are some comments from current and previous students of Rock Valley College:

Student 1: "The staff at Rock Valley does their best to make sure that whatever you choose to study, you will succeed. The staff and professors work their absolute hardest to ensure that you are successful in your education."
Student 2: "As a high school graduate, I had no idea what college I should go to. I decided to stay local, and I am very glad I did so. Rock Valley has a very nice facility and the staff have all been very nice to me. If you are in my situation I would highly consider Rock Valley College!"
Student 3: "I think that if I had to do college over again, I would choose Rock Valley as my first school. It is local, won't break the bank, and offers a level of flexibility in which way you want to take your education. For example, you could get a 2 year certificate, or you could choose to transfer to a 4 year university to get a Bachelor's degree or even a Master's degree. Lots of options available to you, should you choose Rock Valley College.
